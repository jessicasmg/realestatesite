Our Story

About SMG Property

Our Story

SMG Property was founded by Shaul Gabbay over a decade ago with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable housing that helps people feel truly at home.

What began as a small commitment to serve the Aurora community has grown into a portfolio of more than 150 apartments, all managed with the same care, responsiveness, and attention that defined our earliest days.

For us, this work has never been just about buildings or leases. It's about people.

We take pride in seeing individuals and families move into one of our properties, knowing they've found a place that feels safe, stable, and comfortable. That feeling, when someone finally exhales and says "I'm home," is what drives everything we do.

Our Approach

Local & Personal

All of our properties are located within a one-mile radius in Aurora, so when you need maintenance or have a question, we're never far away. Our team responds quickly because we're part of the same community you live in. Your neighborhood is our neighborhood.

Flexible & Fair

Everyone's situation is different, and we understand that. That's why we accept Section 8 housing vouchers and offer multiple payment options, including online, check, and cash payments. About one-third of our residents use Section 8.

At SMG Property, we aim to make housing simple so you can focus on building your life, not worrying about where you'll live.

Our Team

Aurora is one of the most diverse cities in Colorado, and our team proudly reflects that diversity.

We know how important it is for residents, especially immigrants and members of minority communities, to work with a team that understands their experiences and can communicate in their language.

Our staff handles everything that keeps our properties running smoothly: showing available units, processing applications, coordinating maintenance, and being available when you need support. They're not just employees; they're neighbors.

Knowledgeable, responsive, and kind, they're committed to making sure your experience as a resident is positive from day one.

What We Offer

Housing Options

Studios, 1-, 2-, 3-, and 4-bedroom apartments with monthly rent ranging from approximately $900 to $1,600.

Payment Flexibility

Multiple payment methods, including online payments through our tenant portal, traditional checks, and cash payments.

Section 8 Welcome

We gladly accept Section 8 housing vouchers and work closely with housing authorities to make the process seamless.

Responsive Maintenance

Our full-time staff addresses maintenance requests quickly, with a 24-hour emergency line available 365 days a year.

Tenant Portal

Through our partnership with AppFolio, residents can pay rent online, submit maintenance requests, sign lease documents, and view their payment history, all in one convenient place.

Ready to Find Your Home?

At SMG Property, we believe everyone deserves a safe, comfortable, and stable place to live. Whether you're new to Aurora, starting over, or simply looking for housing that fits your budget and lifestyle, we're here to help.

We respond quickly, communicate openly, and maintain properties that residents are proud to call home. Providing housing isn't just a service; it's a way to strengthen our community, one apartment at a time.
