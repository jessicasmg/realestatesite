About SMG Property

Our Story

SMG Property was founded by Shaul Gabbay over a decade ago with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable housing that helps people feel truly at home.

What began as a small commitment to serve the Aurora community has grown into a portfolio of more than 150 apartments, all managed with the same care, responsiveness, and attention that defined our earliest days.

For us, this work has never been just about buildings or leases. It's about people.

We take pride in seeing individuals and families move into one of our properties, knowing they've found a place that feels safe, stable, and comfortable. That feeling, when someone finally exhales and says "I'm home," is what drives everything we do.
