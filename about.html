About Aurora Premier Properties

Building communities, creating homes, changing lives

Our Story

Aurora Premier Properties was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable housing for all residents of Aurora, Colorado. What started as a small property management company has grown into one of the most trusted names in Aurora's rental market.

We believe that everyone deserves a safe, comfortable place to call home, regardless of their financial situation. That's why we've always been proud to accept Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers and work closely with local housing authorities to make homeownership accessible to all.
