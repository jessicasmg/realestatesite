Who We Are

For over 10 years, SMG Property has provided quality, affordable housing to Aurora residents. Led by Shaul Gabbay, we manage more than 150 apartments throughout the city, all within a one-mile radius.

That means when you need us, we're close by and ready to help. Our staff reflects the diversity of Aurora itself, ensuring you'll work with people who understand your needs. We believe everyone deserves a quality place to call home, and we work hard to make that happen for every resident.
