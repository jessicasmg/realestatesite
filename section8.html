Section 8 Program Information

Understanding the Housing Choice Voucher Program

📋 What is Section 8?

The Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program is a federal program that helps eligible low-income families, elderly, and disabled individuals afford decent, safe, and sanitary housing in the private market.

  • Rent assistance based on income
  • Choice of housing location
  • Portable benefits
  • Quality housing standards

💰 How It Works

Participants typically pay 30% of their adjusted monthly income toward rent and utilities. The voucher covers the remaining portion up to a payment standard set by the local housing authority.

  • You pay 30% of adjusted income
  • Voucher covers remaining rent
  • Direct payment to landlord
  • Annual recertification required

🏠 Housing Standards

All Section 8 properties must meet Housing Quality Standards (HQS) to ensure safe, decent, and sanitary living conditions for all tenants.

  • Annual HQS inspections
  • Safe electrical systems
  • Adequate plumbing and heating
  • Structurally sound building

📍 Local Authorities

We work with several housing authorities in the Aurora area to serve voucher holders from multiple jurisdictions.

  • Aurora Housing Authority
  • Adams County Housing Authority
  • Denver Housing Authority
  • Arapahoe County Housing Authority

Our Simple Process

How to rent with your Section 8 voucher

1

Contact Us

Call or email us with your voucher information. We'll help you find available units that meet your needs and are within your voucher payment standard.

2

Schedule Viewing

We'll schedule a tour of available properties that fit your requirements. You can view multiple units to find the perfect match for your family.

3

Submit Application

Complete our rental application along with your Request for Tenancy Approval (RTA) form. We'll process your application promptly.

4

HQS Inspection

We'll coordinate with your housing authority for the required Housing Quality Standards inspection of the property.

5

Lease Signing

Once approved, we'll schedule your lease signing and move-in. Welcome home!
